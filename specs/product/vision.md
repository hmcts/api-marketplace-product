# Product Vision

The API Marketplace is a self-serve product for publishing, discovering, and consuming HMCTS APIs.

## Vision Statement

Enable product teams and consumers to integrate with HMCTS capabilities through reliable, discoverable, and well-governed APIs.

## Goals

- Reduce time to publish and onboard APIs.
- Improve discoverability and consistency of API contracts.
- Provide clear operational ownership and lifecycle controls.

## Non-Goals

- Replacing domain service ownership models.
- Defining implementation details for every service in this repository.
